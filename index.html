Age 19 · Hollywood

Couch to couch

SDM lands in Hollywood as a student and a fly on the wall — sessions with DanjaHandz, Stacy Barthe, Pharrell Williams, Chase N Cashe, Dem Jointz, Christopher Ciccone, Johnny Wright, Omarion, Chris Anokute and more, inside rooms at Westlake, Chalice, Paramount, No Excuses, The Record Plant, and Sunset Sound.

For a year straight he couch‑surfs from studio to studio with no rig of his own, learning to work whatever was already set up in the room — FL Studio, GarageBand, Pro Tools — just to stay in the session. Once he saves up for a laptop and Reason 4, he's back in the saddle. But in those rooms, the last thing anyone needed was more beats — it was SDM's toplines and songwriting that kept him in the room. Enough of it that he'd eventually move back to Texas. Austin, to be exact.
